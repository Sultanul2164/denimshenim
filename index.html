About Us

DENIM SHENIM is a visionary upcycling initiative to help lower the waste production of the Bangladesh RMG industry. This initiative will also help young designers and artists have access to raw materials and a community of other curators that can help bring their vision to life. All while ethically using existing materials to lower our carbon footprint and rethinking our extractive design practices.
